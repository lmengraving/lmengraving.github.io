What We Do

Our Services

Custom marking dies, 3D mold engraving, and industrial laser processing — permanent identification and traceability solutions for the molding industry.

01

Custom Marking Dies & Tooling

Engineering durable steel stamps and rolling dies for permanent part identification and branding applications.

Key Capabilities

Rotary & Roll Dies

Precision-engraved rollers for continuous marking on cylindrical or flat surfaces.

Impact & Press Stamps

Heavy-duty steel stamps for metal stamping and embossing applications.

Hot Stamping Dies

Thermally conductive brass and steel dies for packaging and decorative branding.

02

Industrial Laser Marking & Texturing

Non-contact, permanent marking solutions for traceability, branding, and functional surface texturing.

Key Capabilities

Deep Engraving

High-depth material removal for mold cavities and 3D textures.

Laser Annealing

Corrosion-resistant black marking on stainless steel and titanium (medical/aerospace).

Traceability Systems

Automated QR codes, Data Matrix, and UDI compliance markings.

03

3D Engraving for Molding Applications

Advanced 3D graphics and logos laser engraved on contours for molding applications with advanced 3D layout and machine control.

Key Capabilities

Sidewalls & Contours

5-axis laser ensures no undercuts in line of draw, even for vertical sides, maintaining draft angle for part release.

3D Graphics & Logos

Complex 3D shapes routinely engraved on contoured surfaces where traditional methods fail.

Complex Mold Surfaces

Requiring only straight line of sight, our lasers engrave virtually any complex mold surface geometry.

04

Tool Reference & Mold Traceability

Supporting mold customers with laser marking for component tracking. High cavitation or complex molds require easy reference for maintenance.

Key Capabilities

Component Cataloging

Permanent laser markings (human readable and/or bar codes) for expedited mold refurbishment and assembly.

Part Tracking

Marking mold components for tracking moving parts that require periodic disassembly and maintenance.

Legible Markings

Clear, permanent identification that withstands the rigors of mold operation and cleaning cycles.

05

Electrode Engraving for EDM

Laser engraving of electrodes for EDM applications. Carbon graphite and copper impregnated electrodes etched with precision.

Key Capabilities

Graphite Electrodes

Precision laser etching on carbon graphite electrodes for EDM sinking operations.

Copper Electrodes

Engraving copper and copper-impregnated electrodes with fine detail for complex cavities.

Over-burn Control

Raised or sunk characters with any desired over-burn specification to meet machining requirements.
